# Storyboard: Inside OpenAI's Data Agent

**Art Style**: Manga / Kawaii (Cute, expressive, clean lines)
**Tone**: Energetic, educational, warm.

---

## Page 0: Cover
**Panel 1 (Splash Page)**
- **Visual**: Large, dynamic shot of **Agent-kun** (the cute robot) bursting out of a screen or pile of data blocks, waving hello. **User-chan** is peeking from the side, looking amazed. Title text: "Inside OpenAI's Data Agent".
- **Text**: "How we turned 600PB of data into instant answers!"

---

## Page 1: The Info Overload
**Panel 1**
- **Visual**: **User-chan** sitting at a desk, looking tiny. Behind them is a MONSTER WAVE of file folders and binary code.
- **Text (User)**: "I need to find the launch metrics... but we have 70,000 datasets!!"

**Panel 2**
- **Visual**: Close up of User-chan swirly eyes (dizzy).
- **Text (Thought)**: "Finding the right table alone will take all day... Ugh."

**Panel 3**
- **Visual**: User-chan slams head on desk. (Classic anime defeat).
- **Text**: "I wish I had a data expert sitting right next to me..."

---

## Page 2: Enter the Agent
**Panel 1**
- **Visual**: *POOF!* A cloud of sparkles appears near the desk.
- **SFX**: *KIRA KIRA* (Sparkle sound)

**Panel 2**
- **Visual**: **Agent-kun** emerges! Floating, glowing, striking a hero pose (but cute).
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "Did someone say Data Expert? I'm the Data Agent!"

**Panel 3**
- **Visual**: User-chan looks up, surprised. Agent-kun is offering a hand (or robotic nub).
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "I can search 600PB of data and find your answer in minutes! Just ask me in natural language!"

---

## Page 3: Self-Correction (The "Smart" Part)
**Panel 1**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun wearing a "Detective Hat" looking at a treasure map (data schema).
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "Wait... this path (query) looks wrong. The column name changed!"

**Panel 2**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun trips/falls (cute mistake), but immediately rolls into a somersault to recover.
- **Text (Narrator)**: "Self-Correction Loop: If I hit an error, I don't give up. I investigate and fix it!"

**Panel 3**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun holding up a shiny gem (the correct data).
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "Found it! I adjusted my query and got the right result."

---

## Page 4: Context is Power
**Panel 1**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun showing off its "Backpack of Context". Items are sticking out: a scroll (Docs), a chat bubble (Slack), code snippets.
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "I don't just guess! I use context: Table Lineage, Human Notes, and even Code definitions!"

**Panel 2**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun plugged into a wall labeled "Evals API". It looks buff/strong here (comical).
- **Text**: "And I stay smart using Evals to quality-check every answer!"

---

## Page 5: Trust & Safety
**Panel 1**
- **Visual**: Agent-kun holding a shield marked with a Lock icon. It's protecting User-chan.
- **Dialogue (Agent)**: "Don't worry! I respect all security permissions. I only show you what you're allowed to see."

**Panel 2**
- **Visual**: User-chan looks relieved and happy.
- **Dialogue (User)**: "So you're like a super-smart, secure teammate?"
