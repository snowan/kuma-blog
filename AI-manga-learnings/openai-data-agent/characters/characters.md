# Character Definitions

## 1. Data Agent (Agent-kun)
> A cute, intelligent, digital assistant.

- **Visuals**: A small, floating, spherical robot. Very round and "squishy" looking (Kawaii).
- **Face**: A digital screen that shows big emojis (>_<, ^_^, O_O).
- **Color**: White body with soft pastel teal and purple glowing accents (OpenAI brand-a-like but softer).
- **Accessories**: Occasionally holds a magnifying glass or wears a "detective hat" when searching. Small antenna that wiggles.
- **Style**: Vector/Cel-shaded, clean lines, mascot-like.

## 2. Engineer (User-chan)
> The relatable main character who needs data.

- **Visuals**: Young human, gender-neutral or female.
- **Hair**: Short, slightly messy dark hair (working hard!).
- **Outfit**: Oversized comfortable hoodie (pastel orange or yellow).
- **Expression**: Very expressive manga-style faces (dizzy eyes when confused, sparkly eyes when happy).
- **Style**: Cute, chibi-proportions (2-3 heads tall).

## 3. Background / World
- **Setting**: A clean, high-tech but cozy office space. "Tech startup" vibes but stylized and simplified.
- **Data Representation**: Data is visualized as floating, colorful geometric blocks or glowing orbs, not boring spreadsheets.
