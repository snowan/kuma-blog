## Summary
Aaron Levie argues that AI agents won't replace enterprise software but will expand its market by taking over "work" rather than just providing tools. He distinguishes between "deterministic" software (reliable machinery like ERP) and "non-deterministic" agents (operators), predicting a shift from seat-based to consumption-based pricing.
