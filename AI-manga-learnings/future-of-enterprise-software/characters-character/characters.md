# Character Sheet: Michi (The Agentic Cat)

**Role**: The inquisitive learner and guide.
**Style**: Michi (Cute, Kawaii, Manga-style).
**Appearance**:
-   Calico cat (white with orange and black patches).
-   Large, expressive eyes.
-   Often wears accessories relevant to the topic (e.g., glasses for "analyst mode", hard hat for "factory mode").
-   Simple, clean lines, soft colors (pastels, warm tones).

**Poses**:
-   **Analytic**: Pushing glasses up nose, looking at a chart.
-   **Confused**: Head tilted, question marks.
-   **Excited**: Paws up, sparkles.
-   **Working**: Typing on a laptop or pulling a lever.
