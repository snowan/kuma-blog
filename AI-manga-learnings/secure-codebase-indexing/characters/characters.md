# Characters

## Main Character: Dr. Index (The Guide)
- **Role**: Explainer, expert system architect.
- **Appearance**: A cheerful, sharp-witted engineer avatar.
  - **Style**: Manga anime style.
  - **Features**: Messy enthusiastic hair, round glasses that reflect data streams, wearing a tech-focused hoodie with glowing circuit patterns.
  - **Expression**: Confident, smiling, explanatory.
- **Vibe**: Energetic, helpful, smart.

## Secondary Character: Dev (The User)
- **Role**: Represents the user/reader.
- **Appearance**: Relatable developer.
  - **Style**: Manga anime style.
  - **Features**: Simple t-shirt, looking at a laptop, initially overwhelmed/tired, then happy/relieved.
  - **Vibe**: Relatable, curious.

## Abstract Elements
- **Merkle Tree**: Visualized as a holographic, crystalline tree structure.
- **Data Blocks**: Glowing cubes or puzzle pieces.
