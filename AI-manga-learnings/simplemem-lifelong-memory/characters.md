# Character Definitions

## Michi (The Agent)
**Role**: Protagonist / LLM Agent
**Species**: Calico Cat
**Appearance**: 
- Soft cream/white fur with orange and grey patches.
- Dealing with "Lifelong Memory" requires gear. She wears a lightweight, futuristic "Agent Vest" and a small headset.
- **Expressions**: Determinded, drowsy (when overloaded), wide-eyed (amazed by efficiency).

## Memi (The Memory Module)
**Role**: Sidekick / SimpleMem System
**Species**: Digital Spirit / Sprite
**Appearance**:
- A small, floating, translucent blue sphere with cute digital eyes and small holographic wings.
- Glows when processing data.
- **Personality**: Helpful, efficient, fast.

## Visual Style: Michi
- **Lines**: Clean, hand-drawn ink style.
- **Colors**: Pastel palette with vibrant accents for the "Magic/Tech" elements.
- **Backgrounds**: Simple or abstract to focus on characters and diagrams.
