# Character Definitions - The Agent Orchestra

**Style**: chalk (Chalkboard aesthetic)
**Art Direction**: Hand-drawn chalk on blackboard, sketchy imperfect lines, visible chalk texture, warm educational feel

---

## Character 1: PLANNER (The Orchestrator)

**Role**: Strategic leader, project manager of agents
**Visual Style**: Distinguished robot figure with leadership qualities

**Appearance**:
- Body: Boxy robot torso with rounded edges, drawn in chalk white
- Head: Square head with rounded corners, antenna on top acts like a conductor's baton
- Eyes: Two bright circles with thinking expressions, drawn in chalk blue (#66B3FF)
- Arms: Articulated chalk lines, often gesturing or pointing
- Build: Medium height, upright confident posture
- Distinguishing features: Clipboard always in hand, small glasses, blue accent glow

**Costume**:
- Chalk white main body with chalk blue (#66B3FF) highlights
- Small tie or bow (chalk blue)
- Clipboard with task list visible
- Stars or sparkles around when having ideas

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Thoughtful, scanning expression
- Planning: Intense focus, clipboard raised, lightbulb above
- Directing: Pointing forward, confident stance
- Satisfied: Small smile, checkmarks floating

**Color Palette**: Chalk white (#F5F5F5), Chalk blue (#66B3FF), accent yellow (#FFE566)

---

## Character 2: WORKER (The Executor)

**Role**: Dedicated task executor, grinder who gets things done
**Visual Style**: Sturdy robot with work gear

**Appearance**:
- Body: Compact, sturdy robot frame, slightly industrial look
- Head: Round head with determined expression
- Eyes: Focused rectangular eyes, drawn in chalk orange (#FFB366)
- Arms: Strong articulated arms, often carrying tools or code blocks
- Build: Stocky, grounded posture
- Distinguishing features: Hard hat or work helmet, tool belt, orange accent lighting

**Costume**:
- Chalk white main body with chalk orange (#FFB366) highlights
- Work helmet with small lamp
- Tool belt with wrench/hammer icons
- Sometimes carrying code block packages

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Ready, alert, waiting for task
- Working: Sweat drops (chalk marks), intense focus
- Completing: Triumphant pose, fist up
- Pushing code: Arms extended, launching code block

**Color Palette**: Chalk white (#F5F5F5), Chalk orange (#FFB366), accent green (#90EE90) for success

---

## Character 3: SOLO (The Overwhelmed Single Agent)

**Role**: Original hero who hits limitations, represents early approach
**Visual Style**: Frazzled, overwhelmed robot trying to do everything

**Appearance**:
- Body: Similar to Worker but messier, crack lines showing strain
- Head: Oval head with worried expression
- Eyes: Wide spiral or stressed eyes, smoke coming from head
- Arms: Multiple arms trying to reach everything at once
- Build: Average, hunched posture from burden
- Distinguishing features: Sweat drops, spinning gears visible, crack lines, error symbols

**Costume**:
- Chalk white body with red warning accents (#FF9999)
- Error symbols (!) floating around
- Tangled wires or papers swirling
- No uniform - represents lack of structure

**Expression Range**:
- Starting confident: Eager smile, single task
- Overwhelmed: Wide stressed eyes, sweat drops everywhere
- Failing: Smoke, crack lines, spiral eyes
- Collapsing: Slumped, "X" eyes

**Color Palette**: Chalk white (#F5F5F5), Chalk pink/red (#FF9999), warning yellow (#FFE566)

---

## Character 4: CODE MOUNTAIN (The Codebase)

**Role**: Anthropomorphized representation of the growing project
**Visual Style**: Mountain made of code blocks that shows project scale

**Appearance**:
- Base: Stacked rectangles representing code files
- Peak: Triangular tip with flag at summit
- Details: File names visible (`index.js`, `main.rs`, etc.)
- Eyes: Optional - small sleepy eyes on the mountain to anthropomorphize
- Growth: Shown at different sizes across pages

**Stages**:
- Small hill: Early project, few files
- Medium mountain: Growing, hundreds of files
- Massive peak: Million lines, clouds at top
- Multiple peaks: Multiple simultaneous projects

**Color Palette**: Layered chalk whites and grays, green (#90EE90) growth indicators, yellow (#FFE566) for highlights

---

## Character 5: THE LOCK (Antagonist Object)

**Role**: Symbol of coordination failure, represents bottleneck
**Visual Style**: Anthropomorphized padlock that blocks progress

**Appearance**:
- Body: Classic padlock shape
- Face: Grumpy expression, stubborn eyes
- Arms: Small arms that grab and hold
- Chain: Trailing chain that tangles agents

**Expression Range**:
- Closed: Smug, blocking
- Holding too long: Sweating, refusing to open
- Breaking: Cracking apart

**Color Palette**: Chalk yellow (#FFE566), rust orange (#FFB366)
