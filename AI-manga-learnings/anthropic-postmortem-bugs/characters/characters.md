# Character Definitions: Anthropic Postmortem

**Art Style**: ligne-claire
**Tone**: warm

---

## Main Characters

### Sarah Chen - Lead Infrastructure Engineer

**Role**: Protagonist, investigation leader

**Appearance**:
- Age: Early 30s
- Gender: Female
- Ethnicity: East Asian
- Hair: Black, shoulder-length, often tucked behind ears
- Eyes: Brown, warm and determined
- Height: Average (5'6")
- Build: Slim, professional

**Clothing**:
- Dark blue comfortable sweater or hoodie
- Simple jeans or casual pants
- Anthropic badge on lanyard
- Sometimes wears glasses when debugging

**Expression Range**:
- Default: Focused, slightly concerned
- Investigating: Intense concentration
- Discovery: Eyes widening with realization
- Resolution: Warm, relieved smile

**Personality**: Determined, empathetic, methodical

---

### Marcus Rodriguez - Senior Systems Engineer

**Role**: Team member, hardware specialist

**Appearance**:
- Age: Late 30s
- Gender: Male
- Ethnicity: Hispanic
- Hair: Black, short, neat
- Facial hair: Well-trimmed beard
- Eyes: Dark brown, kind
- Height: Tall (6'1")
- Build: Average

**Clothing**:
- Plaid button-down shirts
- Dark pants
- Often carrying tablet/laptop
- Headphones around neck

**Expression Range**:
- Default: Thoughtful, analytical
- Problem-solving: Furrowed brow
- Explaining: Animated hand gestures
- Success: Broad, warm smile

**Personality**: Knowledgeable, patient, supportive

---

### Priya Sharma - ML Engineer

**Role**: Team member, precision expert

**Appearance**:
- Age: Late 20s
- Gender: Female
- Ethnicity: South Asian
- Hair: Long black hair, usually in ponytail
- Eyes: Dark brown, bright and curious
- Height: Petite (5'3")
- Build: Slim

**Clothing**:
- Graphic tees (tech/math themed)
- Cardigan
- Comfortable sneakers
- Smart watch

**Expression Range**:
- Default: Eager, curious
- Deep thought: Finger on chin
- Eureka moment: Bright smile
- Frustrated: Determined pout

**Personality**: Brilliant, enthusiastic, persistent

---

### Claude (Anthropomorphized)

**Role**: The AI system, affected party

**Appearance**:
- Form: Gentle, ethereal humanoid figure
- Color: Soft orange/coral glow (#E8985E)
- Eyes: Large, warm, circular
- Expression: Kind, gentle
- Aura: Soft warm glow around edges
- Height: Slightly smaller than humans

**Visual Style**:
- Semi-transparent, luminous quality
- Clean ligne-claire outlines
- Gradient glow (only exception to flat colors)
- No harsh edges

**Expression Range**:
- Healthy: Warm, helpful glow
- Affected by bug: Flickering, concerned
- Recovered: Bright, grateful

**Personality**: Helpful, gentle, resilient

---

## Bug Characters

### Bug #1: Routy (Router Bug)

**Appearance**:
- Shape: Round body with direction arrows as tentacles
- Color: Cool blue (#6B8E9F)
- Eyes: Confused, spinning spirals
- Size: Medium

**Visual Elements**:
- Traffic direction arrows pointing wrong ways
- Tangled routing lines
- Question marks floating around

**Behavior**: Misdirects things, looks perpetually confused

---

### Bug #2: Glitchy (Corruption Bug)

**Appearance**:
- Shape: Pixelated, unstable form
- Color: Purple/magenta with glitch effects (#9B59B6)
- Eyes: One larger than other, asymmetric
- Size: Medium, shape-shifting

**Visual Elements**:
- Random characters floating (สวัสดี, 中文)
- Pixel artifacts
- Scrambled text bubbles

**Behavior**: Causes scrambled output, flickering appearance

---

### Bug #3: Decimal (Precision Bug)

**Appearance**:
- Shape: Two misaligned gears
- Color: Mechanical gray with red error indicators (#95A5A6 / #E74C3C)
- Eyes: Calculator-like digital display
- Size: Compact but complex

**Visual Elements**:
- bf16 and fp32 labels on gears
- Decimal points and numbers floating
- Probability charts in background

**Behavior**: Causes precision mismatches, looks mathematical

---
