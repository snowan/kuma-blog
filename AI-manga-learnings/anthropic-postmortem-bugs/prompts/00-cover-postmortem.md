# Cover Page Prompt

**File**: 00-cover-postmortem.png
**Aspect Ratio**: 3:4 (portrait)

## Image Prompt

Create a manga cover page in ligne-claire style with warm tones.

**Title**: "A POSTMORTEM OF THREE RECENT ISSUES"
**Subtitle**: "How Anthropic Hunted Down Three Invisible Bugs"

**Composition**:
- CENTER: Three engineers (Sarah - East Asian woman with black shoulder-length hair in blue sweater, Marcus - Hispanic man with beard in plaid shirt, Priya - South Asian woman with ponytail in graphic tee) standing together, holding magnifying glasses and debugging tools, looking determined yet warm and approachable

- BACKGROUND: Stylized server infrastructure with TPU, GPU, and Trainium chip icons connected by glowing network lines, arranged in an aesthetically pleasing arc behind the characters

- HIDDEN: Three small bug creatures (blue router bug with arrow tentacles, purple glitchy pixelated bug, gray gear-shaped precision bug) hiding among the servers, peeking out mischievously

- TOP: Title text in clean sans-serif font, warm charcoal color
- BOTTOM: Anthropic-style subtle branding, "September 2025"

**Lighting**: Warm golden hour lighting from the side, creating a hopeful atmosphere despite the serious topic

**Color Palette**:
- Background: Cream #FFF8F0
- Primary: Warm blue #4A90A4
- Secondary: Soft orange #E8985E
- Accent: Golden yellow #D69E2E
- Text: Warm charcoal #3D3D3D

**Style Details**:
- Ligne-claire: Clean uniform 2px black outlines on all figures
- Flat colors, no gradients (except subtle glow on servers)
- Detailed background with simplified character faces
- Professional but inviting aesthetic
- Panel-style border with rounded corners
