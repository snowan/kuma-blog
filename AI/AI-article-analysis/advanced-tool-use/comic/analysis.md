- **Role**: The AI trying to use tools effectively

- **Arc**: Struggling → Learning → Mastering

- **Role**: Overwhelming pile of 100+ tools
- **Visual identity**: Mountain of scrolls/folders

- **Tool Search**: Magnifying glass / search icon
