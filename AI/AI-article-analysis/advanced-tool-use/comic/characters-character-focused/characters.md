# Character Definitions - Memory Squad: The Four Graph Heroes

**Style**: michi (kawaii manga)
**Art Direction**: Cute, colorful, expressive chibi characters, big eyes, soft rounded shapes, pastel colors with vibrant accents, sparkle effects, manga expressions

**Role**: Protagonist - AI agent who needs help with memory
**Age**: Young/cute appearance

## Character 2: Sema-chan (Semantic Graph)

**Role**: Team member - finds similar concepts
**Personality**: Gentle, wise, dreamy

**Appearance**:
- Form: Fluffy cloud-like being with constellation patterns
- Body: Soft, rounded, cloud-textured
- Eyes: Large gentle blue eyes with star reflections
- Color: Soft cyan blue (#87CEEB) with darker blue (#4682B4) constellation dots
- Distinguishing features: Star freckles on cheeks, constantly has little stars floating around

**Costume**:
- Natural form: Cloud body with sparkly constellation pattern inside
- Accessories: Crown of tiny connected stars

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Serene smile, half-lidded eyes
- Connecting: Eyes light up, constellation pattern glows
- Shy/Uncertain: Shrinks smaller, sweat drop
- Proud: Puffs up larger, sparkles intensify

## Character 4: Causa-san (Causal Graph)

**Role**: Team member - traces cause and effect

- Body: More triangular/diamond shaped than others
- Eyes: Sharp orange eyes with magnifying glass monocle

- Tiny detective coat/cape
- Magnifying glass that glows when detecting cause-effect
- Accessories: Notepad with arrow drawings

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Cool collected look, slight smirk
- Investigating: Monocle glows, leaning forward

## Character 5: Enti-chan (Entity Graph)

**Role**: Team member - remembers entities across time
**Personality**: Warm, caring, excellent memory for names

**Appearance**:
- Form: Hub-shaped with extending ribbons
- Body: Central circular hub with radiating connection ribbons
- Eyes: Large warm purple eyes with heart pupils
- Color: Purple (#805AD5) with lavender (#E6E6FA) ribbons
- Distinguishing features: Name tags floating around, ribbons stretch to connect things

**Costume**:
- Central body decorated with tiny icons (people, places, things)
- Extending ribbons that can reach out and connect
- Accessories: Friendship bracelets with entity labels

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Welcoming smile, ribbons gently floating
- Connecting: Ribbons stretch out, eyes focused
- Remembering: Light bulb effect, happy recognition
- Hugging: Ribbons wrap around friends warmly

**Catchphrases**: "I remember you! üíú" "Everyone is connected!"

## Character 6: Intent Router (Routa)

**Role**: Supporting - directs queries to correct team member
**Personality**: Efficient, helpful, traffic controller vibes

**Appearance**:
- Form: Small cube character with directional arms
- Body: Compact cube with screen face
- Eyes: Digital display eyes, show arrows when directing
- Color: White with multicolor directional indicators
- Distinguishing features: Traffic controller hat, glowing pointing arms

**Costume**:
- Cute traffic controller outfit
- Four-color directional flags matching team colors
- Whistle accessory

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Alert, scanning
- Directing: Points enthusiastically to correct team member
- Success: Checkmark eyes, thumbs up

**Catchphrases**: "This way! ‚Üí" "Let me connect you to the right friend!"
