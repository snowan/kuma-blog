## üìö Article Summary

This article explores how **Context Graphs** represent the next trillion-dollar platform opportunity in enterprise AI. Traditional "systems of record" (like Salesforce, Workday, SAP) captured *what* data exists, but they miss a critical layer: *why* decisions were made. 

As AI agents become more autonomous, they need decision traces‚Äîrecords of not just outcomes, but the context, policies, exceptions, and precedents behind each decision. Startups that sit in the "orchestration layer" where decisions actually happen have a structural advantage to capture this context and build the next generation of systems of record.
