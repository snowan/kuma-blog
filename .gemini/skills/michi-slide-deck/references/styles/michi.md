# michi

Simple cute calico cat in manga style - Michi's signature aesthetic for presentations

## Color Palette

- Primary: Cream white (#FFFEF2), soft orange (#FFD8A8), warm brown (#D4A574), charcoal (#4A4A4A)
- Background: Warm cream (#FFF8E7), soft paper texture
- Accents: Blush pink (#FFB6C1), gentle coral (#FF7F7F), soft sky blue (#87CEEB)

## Visual Elements

- Cute calico cat mascot (Michi) as presenter/guide
- Simple manga-style expressions throughout slides
- Soft hand-drawn lines, sketch-like quality
- Kawaii aesthetic with simplicity
- Cat in different poses: pointing, thinking, explaining, celebrating
- Speech bubbles for key points
- Paw prints as bullet points
- Soft shadows, gentle gradients

## Typography

- Clean hand lettering in English
- Rounded, friendly fonts
- Good readability with warmth

## Slide Layouts

- Cat mascot in corner or side as guide
- Hand-drawn frames and borders
- Sticker-style callouts
- Cozy, notebook-like feel

## Animation Feel

- Gentle, cozy, inviting atmosphere
- Studio Ghibli-inspired warmth
- Makes complex topics approachable

## Best For

- Educational presentations
- Team meetings with friendly vibe
- Personal project showcases
- Tech talks that need approachability
